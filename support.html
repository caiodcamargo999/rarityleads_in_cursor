Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I set up my first campaign?

Setting up your first campaign is easy:

  1. Go to Dashboard and click "New Campaign"
  2. Choose your target sector (SaaS, Finance, Healthcare, etc.)
  3. Set your lead criteria (company size, revenue, location)
  4. Connect your WhatsApp account(s)
  5. Create your message templates
  6. Launch and monitor your campaign

Our AI will automatically find and qualify leads based on your criteria.

How many WhatsApp accounts can I connect?

The number of WhatsApp accounts depends on your plan:

  • Starter: Up to 2 WhatsApp accounts
  • Professional: Up to 5 WhatsApp accounts
  • Enterprise: Unlimited WhatsApp accounts

Each account can be managed from a single dashboard with easy switching between numbers.

What's the difference between warm and cold leads?

Warm leads are prospects who have shown intent signals such as:

  • Recent hiring activity
  • Funding announcements
  • Technology stack changes
  • Website activity

Cold leads are prospects with no recent activity but match your ICP criteria. Our AI prioritizes warm leads for higher conversion rates.

Features & Functionality

How does the AI lead scoring work?

Our AI analyzes 500+ data points to score leads:

  • Firmographics: Company size, revenue, industry
  • Technographics: Technology stack, tools used
  • Behavioral signals: Website activity, social engagement
  • Intent signals: Recent hiring, funding, expansion

Leads are scored 0-100, with 80+ being highly qualified prospects ready to engage.

Can I integrate with my existing CRM?

Yes! We support two-way sync with major CRMs:

  • HubSpot: Full integration with contact sync
  • Pipedrive: Deal and contact management
  • Salesforce: Enterprise-level integration
  • Custom APIs: Available for Enterprise plans

Leads and conversation history automatically sync to your CRM.

How does phone number validation work?

We validate phone numbers through multiple methods:

  1. WhatsApp Business API: Checks if number is registered
  2. Third-party validation: Numverify, Twilio Lookup
  3. Real-time verification: Active status checking

Numbers are categorized as "WhatsApp-enabled" or "SMS-only" for optimal outreach.

Billing & Plans

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time:

  • Downgrades: Take effect at the next billing cycle

Contact support for custom Enterprise arrangements.

No credit card required to start your trial!

Technical Support

My WhatsApp messages aren't sending. What should I do?

If messages aren't sending, try these steps:

  1. Check your WhatsApp account status in Settings
  2. Verify your phone number is validated
  3. Ensure you haven't exceeded daily limits
  4. Check if the recipient number is valid
  5. Restart your campaign if needed

If issues persist, contact support with your campaign details.
